About Alice Jenkins

Marketing strategist • Story evangelist • Data nerd

From Big 5 Boardrooms to Indie Bestseller Lists

I spent seven years inside a Manhattan publicity powerhouse, crafting campaigns for Penguin Random House, HarperCollins and Simon & Schuster. I learned how the giants secure Today Show segments, NYT features, and coveted “Best Of” spots and I perfected the art of replicating those results for authors outside the traditional gatekeepers.

Since 2018 my boutique agency has helped 1,200+ authors sell more than 4.8 million books. Genres range from spicy rom com to cerebral sci-fi, yet every campaign is rooted in the same principles: reader psychology, platform algorithms, and irresistible storytelling.

Why Authors Trust Me

  • Direct relationships with 3,400+ bookstagrammers, TikTok influencers & librarians.
  • 24-hour response promise your questions never sit in an inbox.
